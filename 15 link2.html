Troops

In Clash of Clans, troops are offensive units used to attack enemy bases in Multiplayer Battles, Clan Wars, a single-player campaign, and Clan Capital raids. They are trained in various barracks (Elixir, Dark Elixir, Builder, and Clan Capital) and stored in Army Camps.

Barbarian

Close-range attacker with balanced stats.

Archer

Ranged unit useful for picking off defenses.

Giant

High health; targets defenses first.

Dragon

Flying unit with powerful splash damage.

P.E.K.K.A

Extremely high damage and durability.
